Shipping & Returns

As per the state of the current world and the difficulty in obtaining raw materials, our item-delivery days on some (not all) of our products have been extended to between 5-14 days.

All deliveries should arrive in the earlier part of the time-frame, but it is completely out of our hands.

Tracking information is available for most products.

Unfortunately we don’t offer free postal returns – you’ll have to pay for the postage unless you’re returning faulty or damaged items.

Once we've received your parcel back at our warehouse, please allow 14 days for your refund to arrive. We'll get in touch when your refund has been processed.

Don’t forget to obtain proof of posting, and please allow at least 5 business days for your goods to reach us.

We can’t accept responsibility for items that are lost or damaged in the post.
